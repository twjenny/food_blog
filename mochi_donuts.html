Mochi Donut

  • Ingredients: mochi powder, donut making ingredients :)
  • Place: California, Sacramento
  • Jen's Rating:

~Recommendations~

  1. Since these donuts are very sweet, eat them with coffee! I personally like my drinks cold, so I would like ice coffee, but hot coffee works just as same.
  2. Milk is also a good alternative for coffee!!
  3. I like matcha mochi the most since the matcha flavor goes well with chewiness of the mochi donut!
