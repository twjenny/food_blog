Pistachio Shaved Ice

  • Ingredients: shaved condensed milk ice, crushed pistachio nuts, pistachio syrup, honey covered almonds
  • Place: South Korea, Gangnam
  • Jen's Rating:

~Recommendations~

  1. Try eating this shaved ice from top to bottom! There are pistachio syrup distributed throughout the shaved ice, so no need to worry about runnning out of flavor!
  2. Make sure to get the pistachio syrup on EVERY BITE!! Every bite is important because this shaved ice is VERY VERY good:)
  3. It is a little ironic, but this shaved ice goes very well with iced coffee! Pistachio shaved ice gets a little too heavy towards the end, but a sip of ice coffee refreshes your mouth!
