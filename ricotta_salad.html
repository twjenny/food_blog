Ricotta Cheese Salad

  • Ingredients: fresh leafy salad, ricotta cheese, grape tomatoes, raisin, sliced almonds, balsamic sauce, soft baguette
  • Place: South Korea, Seoul
  • Jen's Rating:

~Recommendations~

Salad sounds boring? Well, it is not as boring as it seems:)

  1. Spray balsamic sauce little by little! If you pour the whole sauce at one go, then the bottom salad won't get any sauce...it's very sad..
  2. Try eating the salad with potato soup! While salad gives a light feeling, potato soup brings down the weight of the food, making it well balanced!
  3. Try useing the ricotta cheese like a spread and paste it on the bread! It is definitely lighter than cream cheese, but it gives another wonderful taste.
  4. I like to eat ricotta salad with some fruity drinks! BUT make sure that the drink is not too sweet because it will overpower the sweetness of the raisins :(
