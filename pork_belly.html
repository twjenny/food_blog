Pork Belly

  • Ingredients: pork belly, salt, papper, lettuce, ssamjang, garlic, rice
  • Place: South Korea, goen-ji-am
  • Jen's Rating:

~Recommendations~

  1. To have a smoky flavor, cook on charoal heat!
  2. Season lightly with salt and pepper, and cook until the pork is fully cooked.
  3. If you want to try eating pork belly in Korean style, try the lettuce wrap! You put a piece of meat, slice of garlic, rice, and ssamjang (seasoned soy bean sauce)!
